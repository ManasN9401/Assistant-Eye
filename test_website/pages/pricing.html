Frequently asked questions

What counts as a "user" for billing purposes?
A user is any person who can log in to your NovaSuite workspace, including admins, members, and guests with edit access. Read-only guests are free. Billing is per seat, per month, charged to the workspace owner. Users added mid-cycle are prorated. Users removed mid-cycle receive credit toward the next invoice. Guest accounts with comment-only access do not count as seats on Pro and above, but do on Starter.
Can I mix plans across team members?
No. All members of a workspace share the same plan tier. If you need different access levels, you can use multiple workspaces. Note that projects cannot be shared across workspaces without an Enterprise plan and the cross-workspace feature add-on ($12/user/month).
How does the annual discount work?
Annual plans are billed as a single upfront payment for 12 months. The discount is applied to the per-user-per-month rate. Pro annual is $15/user/month (vs $18 monthly). Business annual is $39/user/month (vs $49 monthly). Refunds for annual plans are available within 30 days of purchase. After 30 days, a prorated credit is issued for unused months.
What happens when I exceed storage?
You'll receive email warnings at 80% and 95% of your storage limit. On Starter, uploads are blocked at 100%. On paid plans, you can purchase additional storage in 50GB increments for $8/month. Existing files are never deleted automatically — you'll just be unable to upload new files until storage is expanded or existing files are removed.
Is there a non-profit or educational discount?
Yes. Verified non-profits and educational institutions receive 50% off Pro and Business plans. Enterprise pricing for qualifying organizations is negotiated separately. Contact our team with your organization's documentation to apply. Processing typically takes 5–7 business days.
